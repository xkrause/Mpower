M-Power Youth Mission


To provide academic or economic disadvantaged youth a path to becoming self-confident, productive citizens through participation in the arts.

M-Power Youth is a community led partnership of professional and volunteer musicians.
It seeks to transform the lives of youth by providing instructional time in music based on the successful program "El Sistema" (CBS-60 minutes-April 17, 2008) in a welcoming and safe environment beyond their regular school day.

The goal is to enable youth to expand their education, world view and feeling of self-worth in order to become productive members of the community.

M-Power Youth


Providing students a solid foundation in:

Improving self-confidence!!

Learning to play music - instruments provided!!
Earning rewards

Enjoying after-school snacks!!

Interacting with Mentors!!

Hearing from various professionals about career pathways!!

The Program will be conducted after school on Monday, Tuesday, and Friday at the Kent Lutheran Church Annex for two hours per day during the school year and during a three week summer camp in August.
